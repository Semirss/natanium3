Natanem Wondwosen is a young and enthusiastic Ethiopian designer who is making significant strides both within Ethiopia and on the international stage. Driven by her passion and creative mind, she has dedicated her time and effort to creating impactful designs. Her work reflects a commitment to innovation and a desire to showcase Ethiopian talent and artistry to a wider audience.

Natanem aims to not only elevate the design landscape in Ethiopia but also to establish a strong presence globally. Her dedication to her craft and her enthusiastic approach are evident in the quality and originality of her creations, positioning her as a promising force in the design world.
